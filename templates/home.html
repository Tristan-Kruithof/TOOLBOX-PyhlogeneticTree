What is a Phylogenetic Tree and why would I want one?

A Phylogenetic Tree is a powerful biological tool that can help show evolutionary relationships and history between different species. It is very useful when comparing different species or discovering new species. It can help answer questions such as: "To what species is this new unknown species related?", "What is the most recent ancestor to these two different species/" or even "To what extent are these seamingly similar species actually related?" Getting answers to these questions can help identify new diseases and help research into the right direction or help explain biological similarities or differences between species. When styled correctly it's easy to follow and understand, even for people without a background in Biology.
